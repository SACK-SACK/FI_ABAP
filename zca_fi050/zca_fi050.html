INITIALIZATION.

AT SELECTION-SCREEN.

AT SELECTION-SCREEN OUTPUT.
  LOOP AT SCREEN.
    IF screen-group1 = 'FIX'. " 위의 MODIF ID fix와 일치
      screen-input = 0.      " 입력 불가
      screen-output = 1.     " 화면 표시
      MODIFY SCREEN.
    ENDIF.
  ENDLOOP.


START-OF-SELECTION.

  PERFORM select_data.
  PERFORM make_display_data.
  PERFORM display_data.
